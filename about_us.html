About Automated Delights

Automated Delights Machines in Our Business

Watch video

Automated Delights is a leading provider of vending services for businesses and organizations in the United States. Founded in 2020 by Dominique Hosea, the company has thrived on the principle of providing quality products and excellent customer service.

In keeping with their vision of innovation and customer satisfaction, Automated Delights uses the latest, state-of-the-art vending machines, which offer advanced features such as touchscreens, cashless payment options, and remote inventory monitoring. This ensures that their vending machines are always well-stocked, with fresh and quality products.

The company prides itself on its exceptional customer service, which includes timely delivery, efficient restocking, and proactive maintenance. Their team of experienced technicians is always available to handle any machine-related issues, ensuring minimal downtime and disruption for their clients.

Automated Delights understands the evolving needs of modern businesses and organizations and has customized their services accordingly. They offer tailored vending solutions, including freestanding machines, micro-market systems, and office coffee services to meet their clients' requirements.

As a socially conscious business, Automated Delights supports local communities by sourcing products from local suppliers and donating a percentage of their profits to charitable causes.

Their commitment to excellence has earned Automated Delights a loyal clientele, including businesses in healthcare, education, government, and finance sectors. With continued growth and innovation, Automated Delights looks forward to expanding its services and serving more clients across the country.
